About.
This site was created during the ongoing 2023 Israeli aggression in the Gaza Strip in which more than 15,000 Palestinians have been murdered and millions displaced. Looking for a way to counteract these atrocities, we were inspired by the actions of activists in Tacoma, Oakland, Port Botany, and worldwide. Here dock workers and activists were able to delay ships believed to be carrying arms to Israel. This site is meant to give more activists the tools to find these ships and stop them.
