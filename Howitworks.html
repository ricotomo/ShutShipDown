How it Works.
This section explains how activists can locate and track ships that may be involved in transporting arms. Here’s how it works:
  1. Access our "Ships" database: Our database provides a list of over 50 vessels that may be used for arms transport.
  2. Track ships near you: Use our "Bot" feature to receive notifications when any listed ships arrive at a port near your location.
  3. Take Action: Learn about the various ways to protest or delay ships, inspired by dock workers and activists worldwide.
This site aims to empower activists by offering tools to find, track, and take action against these ships.
